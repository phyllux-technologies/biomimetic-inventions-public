PHYLLUX WAVE

Golden-Angle Phyllotactic Antenna Array (GAFAA)

Antenna arrays utilizing phyllotactic element positioning achieve superior performance characteristics compared to conventional grid-based designs. The golden-angle spacing pattern reduces mutual coupling between elements while maximizing spatial efficiency.

  • 121-node phyllotactic geometry with position 61 phase reference
  • Reduced mutual coupling between antenna elements
  • Superior packing density and coverage uniformity
  • Scalable across frequency bands and array sizes
  • Flexible substrate compatibility

Target Applications

  • Satellite communications (LEO, MEO, GEO)
  • Phased-array radar systems
  • 5G/6G network infrastructure
  • Deep-space communications
  • Aerospace and defense systems
Technical Specifications
  • Operating frequency range: 1–100 GHz (scalable)
  • Element count: 121-node reference design (scalable to 89, 233, 377+ nodes)
  • Spacing: Golden angle (137.508°) radial arrangement
  • Mutual coupling: <-20dB (phyllotactic spacing advantage)
  • Beamforming: 360° azimuth, ±60° elevation coverage
  • Scalability: Linear scaling from handheld to satellite arrays
  • Substrate compatibility: FR-4, Rogers, flexible PCB, 3D-printed

  • Optimal coverage uniformity across target regions
  • Minimal spatial redundancy and blind spots
  • Reduced electrode count for equivalent coverage
  • Biocompatible materials and flexible substrates
  • Scalable density for different applications

Target Applications

  • Brain-computer interfaces (motor/sensory)
  • Neural recording and stimulation
  • Medical diagnostics (epilepsy, Parkinson's)
  • Neural prosthetics and restoration
  • Research neuroscience platforms

  • Structured entropy generation using golden-angle rotations
  • Deterministic reproducibility from seed values
  • Position 61 anchor point for key initialization
  • Scalable keyspace complexity (121-position architecture)
  • No backdoors or hidden access mechanisms

Target Applications

  • Military and defense cryptography
  • Secure key generation and management
  • Financial transaction security
  • Secure communications infrastructure
  • Blockchain and distributed systems
Technical Specifications
  • Key generation: Golden angle rotation sequences (137.508° phase space)
  • Entropy source: Phyllotactic indexing (unpredictable natural pattern)
  • Algorithm complexity: O(n log n) generation, O(1) verification
  • Keyspace: 2256+ equivalent (quantum-resistant scale)
  • Implementation: Hardware-friendly (no complex floating-point)
  • Attack resistance: Immune to side-channel timing attacks (constant-time ops)
  • Standards compatibility: Can wrap existing protocols (TLS, SSH, etc.)

The Phyllux Framework

Ethical Technology Licensing for Mission-Aligned Innovation

Phyllux Technologies employs a tiered licensing framework that rewards mission alignment, ethical application, and collaborative innovation while maintaining commercial viability.

🏆 Pioneer

Pioneer Tier

Benefits

  • Equity participation opportunity (co-ownership in specific applications)
  • Joint patent filing on derivative inventions
  • Preferential royalty rates (0.5–1%)
  • First access to new developments
  • Technical co-development support
  • Public recognition as Pioneer partner

Requirements

  • Significant technical contribution to Phyllux platform
  • Shared R&D investment
  • Mission-aligned values
  • Long-term partnership commitment

Example: Co-developing next-generation BCI or optimizing satellite array architecture

🚀 Mission-Aligned

Mission-Aligned Tier

Benefits

  • Reduced royalty rates (1–3%)
  • Priority technical support
  • Flexible payment terms
  • Collaborative roadmap input
  • Joint marketing opportunities

Requirements

  • Humanitarian, medical, or space exploration focus
  • Demonstrable societal benefit
  • Ethical application commitment
  • No dual-use for offensive weapons

Example: Medical BCI for paralysis treatment or deep space communications for NASA missions

💼 Commercial

Commercial Tier

Benefits

  • Clear territorial licensing
  • Standard royalty rates (3–5%)
  • Technical documentation access
  • Implementation support
  • Patent protection

Requirements

  • Ethical use compliance
  • Standard commercial terms
  • Quality standards adherence
  • Reporting requirements

Example: 5G base station optimization or automotive radar systems

🎓 Research

Academic & Research Tier

Benefits

  • FREE for non-commercial research
  • Full access to open-source framework
  • Citation requirements only
  • Research collaboration opportunities
  • Publication rights

Requirements

  • Non-commercial use
  • Proper attribution
  • Publication of results (open science)
  • No patent filing on Phyllux-derived work

Example: University antenna research or neuroscience electrode studies

    Built on Ethical Foundations

    Technology development guided by clear moral boundaries and humanitarian priorities

    🛡️

    Defense, Not Offense

    Phyllux technology will not be licensed for weapons systems, autonomous kill chains, or offensive military applications. Defensive systems, protective technologies, and non-lethal applications are within scope.

    👁️

    Privacy & Human Rights

    No licensing for mass surveillance, abusive monitoring, or technologies that enable oppression. Applications must respect individual privacy and human dignity.

    🔓

    Transparent Security

    All cryptographic systems are free from backdoors and hidden access mechanisms. Security through transparency and mathematical rigor, not through obscurity or covert access.
